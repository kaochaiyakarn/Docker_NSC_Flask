SLEEP STAGE SCORING


"

Sleep Stage Scoring are consists of five stages
including Non-REM(NREM) Sleep(Stage 0,1,2,3),
and REM sleep. Periods of wakefulness occur
before and intermittently throughout the various
sleep stages or as one shifts sleeping position.


"



STAGE OF SLEEP






Stages 0


Eyes open, responsive to external stimuli and it can hold intelligible conversation

Stages 1


Your body starts preparing for deep sleep. Eye movements and brain waves slow down, your body temperature drops, and your heart rate slows down

Stages 2


The first actual stage of define NREM sleep. Awakenings or arousals do not occur as easily as in stage1 sleep and slow-moving eye rolls discontinued. Brain waves continues to slow with specific bursts of rapid activity known as sleep spindles intermixed

Stages 3


Sometimes also called ’NREM Sleep’ The most restorative stage of sleep consists of delta waves or slow waves

Stages REM


REM Sleep also known as rapid eye movement, is most commonly known as the dreaming stage. Eye movements are rapid, moving from side to side and brain waves are more active than in stage 2-3 of sleep.
